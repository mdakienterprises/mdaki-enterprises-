About Us

Welcome to Mdaki Entreprises (ME) – where the essence of Tanzania’s nature meets unmatched quality.

Located in Morogoro-Tanzania.

Our family-owned business is passionate about delivering honey that’s pure, natural, and simply irresistible.

Our honey isn’t just a product; it’s a journey from our vibrant forests to your table.

With every jar of Tausi Asali Mbichi (TAM) and Baraka Asali Ghafi (BAG), you’re tasting the very best of what Tanzania has to offer.
